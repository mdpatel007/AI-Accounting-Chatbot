An invoice is a document issued by a business to a customer that lists the products or services 
provided and the amount the customer must pay.

Invoices play an important role in bookkeeping and financial tracking because they provide 
a formal record of sales transactions.

A typical invoice includes:
- The name of the business issuing the invoice
- Customer information
- A description of goods or services
- Quantity and price
- Total amount due
- Payment terms and due date

Businesses use invoices to track payments, manage accounts receivable, and maintain 
accurate financial records.
