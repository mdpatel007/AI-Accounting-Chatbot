Bookkeeping is the process of recording and organizing the financial transactions of a business. 
It is one of the most fundamental activities in accounting and helps businesses keep track of 
their income, expenses, and overall financial health.

Every time a business makes a sale, purchases supplies, pays salaries, or spends money on 
operations, those transactions should be recorded through bookkeeping.

Bookkeeping typically includes activities such as:
- Recording daily financial transactions
- Maintaining ledgers
- Tracking income and expenses
- Managing invoices and receipts

Proper bookkeeping helps businesses prepare financial statements, monitor cash flow, 
and make better financial decisions.
